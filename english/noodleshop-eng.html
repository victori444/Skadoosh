Our Allies

To make something special you just have to believe it is special. Everyone has that glimpse of a warrior. Now is your chance to prove that you are worthy of being one.

On behalf of Marcelline Robotics, we would like to express our sincerest thanks to Caisse Desjardins de Bordeaux-Cartierville-Saint-Laurent that gave us the support, that pushed us to always aim for the best results so as not to disappoint our fair contributors and motivators, thus we sought to consistently surpass our limits. This year, the team at Caisse Desjardins de Bordeaux-Cartierville-Saint-Laurent gave us funding that’s going to help us defeat Invicta.

For many years, Dejardins has been helping us and we hope that we can keep doing what we do and reach even greater goals as well as winning bigger battles in the future. We hope to live up to the expectations from the previous years, because without their contribution we would not have been able to achieve our purpose.
