Marcy’s palace

Welcome to Marcy’s palace. In your world, you call it Sainte-Marcelline College. This school is home to our brightest, renowned warriors.

Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift. That is why it is called present. Marcy’s palace will guide you and teach you all of it’s great values.

We brought you here to help us defeat a threat that has been around for years - Invicta! During your journey in the valley of peace, you’ll sharpen your skills to become a great warrior. You’ll be able to train and gain both knowledge and power to defeat Invicta.

Marcy’s Palace is an institution with a tremendously warm, family-like, atmosphere that many warriors have called home since 1967. Students from different parts of the world entered its doors and walked its hallways, making diversity one of its many qualities. We will guide you through your journey and we wish you the best of luck.
